Florence Nightingale Foundation Academy Quality Improvement Study

Estimated completion time: 8-10 minutes | All responses are confidential
Study Information

📋 About This Study

Study Aim: To reduce self-reported burnout levels among OSRU ward staff by at least 20% over a two-month period through targeted wellbeing interventions.

What's involved:

  • Complete this baseline assessment (8-10 minutes)
  • Receive personalized wellbeing recommendations
  • Complete a follow-up assessment after 2 months

🔒 Your Privacy & Data Protection

  • Anonymous: No names or personally identifiable information collected
  • Confidential: Individual responses will not be shared with management
  • Secure: Data stored securely and used only for this research study
  • Optional: You can skip any question or withdraw at any time
  • GDPR Compliant: All data handling follows NHS and UK data protection standards

📊 Assessment Tools Used

This survey uses validated burnout assessment questions adapted from:

  • Copenhagen Burnout Inventory (CBI) - validated for healthcare workers
  • Maslach Burnout Inventory principles - measuring emotional exhaustion, depersonalization, and personal accomplishment
  • Additional questions on organizational support and work environment
